Frequently asked questions

Answers to the most common questions

Is my information secure?

Yes. Data is encrypted and stored in Firebase. It is not sold or used for advertising.

Can I change the language?

Yes. The app is available in Catalan, Spanish, English, Basque, and Galician.

Does it work for personal and family use?

Yes. It is ideal for personal or household expenses with categories and totals.

Can I attach receipt photos?

Yes. You can attach an image to every expense.

How can I export my data?

You can export all your expenses from the app at any time. Perfect for tracking in a spreadsheet or doing your accounting.
