Frequently asked questions

Common questions about Onticket

Is my financial data secure?

Yes. All data is encrypted and securely stored in Google Firebase. We do not sell your data or use it for targeted advertising.

Can I use this for family budgeting?

Absolutely. Onticket is designed for both personal finance and family budget tracking, allowing you to filter expenses by family member.

Does it support receipt scanning?

Yes. You can attach a high-quality photo to every expense entry, making it easy to store digital receipts for warranties or tax purposes.

Is the app free to use?

Onticket offers core expense tracking features for free. We also offer premium features for advanced users.

How can I export my expense reports?

You can export your complete spending history to CSV or Excel compatible formats at any time. ideal for home accounting or tax preparation.
