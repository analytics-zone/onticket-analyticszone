Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 16, 2025

1. Introduction

At onticket, your privacy is our priority. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application and our website.

2. Data We Collect

To provide you with the expense tracking service, we need to process the following data:

  • Account Information: Email and password (encrypted) for secure authentication via Firebase Auth.
  • Expense Data: Titles, amounts, dates, categories, and images (receipts) that you voluntarily upload.
  • Technical Data: Basic device information for error management and performance.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your information exclusively to:

  • Provide the app functionality (save and view your expenses).
  • Sync data between your devices in the cloud.
  • Improve user experience and fix bugs.

We DO NOT sell or share your personal data with third parties for advertising purposes.

4. Data Security

Your data is stored securely on Google Firebase servers. We use encrypted connections (SSL/TLS) for all communications between your device and the server.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data.
  • Rectify incorrect data.
  • Delete your account and all your data directly from the app (Settings > Delete Account).
  • Export your data in standard formats (CSV/Excel).

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, you can contact us at: analyticszone@onmedic.org.
